Would you beta test games for a greater cause?

Were you in a position to test beta versions of games for free and by doing so help many others, would you do so?

This could take the form of reporting bugs as GitHub issues, playing specific parts of games to replicate bugs reported by others, and device testing games on whichever devices you have access to.
